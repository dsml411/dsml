Introduction

Prof. Steve S. Chung is the investigator of our research team . The lab is located on Room 411, Engineering Building IV, National Chiao Tung University, Hsinchu City.
Recently, our source of funding and the cooperative partners include NSC, ERSO, TSMC, UMC and MOSEL etc.
Over the years, the graduates from our lab mainly do the jobs about electronics industry. They play vital roles in the IC companies in science park such as the research, design and productive deparment of TSMC, UMC, Winbond and Macronix etc.

Over the past five years,the research projects includes:

  • development of TFET、FinFET device structure and technique
  • development of flash memory、RRAM、OTP device and circuit system
  • Research of reliability analysis methods of above-mentioned devices
  • Establishment of SPICE models of devices

This year, the researches of forefront devices that are our main research topics include:

  • fabrication of nano devices and the researches of models and physical mechanism
  • the researches of reliability measurement technique about high permittivity(like: high-K,SION) CMOS devices
  • the scaling, latest operating mode and , reliability researches of flash memory
  • the researches of noise and Spice models of high frequency wireless communication CMOS devices
