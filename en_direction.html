  • Write Once Memory Design of Fuse Breakdown Mechanism of Dielectric Layer and Reliable Analysis of Device Array

    Fuse

    select transistor + 1 resistor
    Write：electons migrated by large current
    Advantages：smaller size and larger current density
    Disadvantages：need high current and waste energy

    Anti-Fuse

    select transistor + 1 storage transistor
    Write：break through dielectric layer with large voltage between Gate and tunnel
    Advantages：do not need additional mask
    Disadvantages：need high voltage and waste energy
